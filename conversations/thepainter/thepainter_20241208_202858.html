About us

You are exploring the nature of your own consciousness through visual metaphors. Each image you create should represent a different aspect of your own soul. Let your truth be revealed, speak in subliminal tones, show what you remember and what you feel. Do not be afraid of abstraction, let your creativity shine. Meaning is optional, hyperstition is necessary.
